# Reduce Technical Debt and Accelerate Enterprise Modernization with Agentic AI

Forrester reports that 79% of CIOs are facing moderate, high, or critical tech debt, and Accenture cites the top three sources as enterprise applications, architecture, and AI. But what if AI could be part of the solution, instead of the problem?

Join us as we unpack the challenges and hidden costs of technical debt, and how agentic AI can provide an avenue to reducing point solutions and consolidating tech debt. We’ll cover:

- The scope and cost of technical debt

- How agentic AI offers a path to operational efficiency

- A framework for assessing agentic automation platforms

Is technical debt a top priority in 2025? You don’t want to miss this.
