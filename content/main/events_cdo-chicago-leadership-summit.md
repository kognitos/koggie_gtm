# CDO Chicago Leadership Summit

Chicago, Illinois

Kognitos is proud to participate in the CDO Chicago Leadership Summit, a premier gathering of C-level executives, data leaders, and innovators. Throughout the day, attendees will dive deep into emerging trends, data analytics, and cutting-edge approaches to data governance, focusing on these top data trends:

- Data Foundations for AI

- Data Strategy & Leadership

- The Convergence of Data, AI, and Ethics in the Modern Enterprise

- Talent Management, Culture, and Organizational Change Management

- Associating Data & AI to Business Outcomes.

Kognitos’ VP of Solutions Engineering, Neeraj Mathur, will lead a highly engaging focus group session onClosing the AI Action Gap with Governance and Trustexploringnew perspectives on how to accelerate results, strengthen governance, and envision a future where AI-powered processes can be described, automated, and audited in plain English.
