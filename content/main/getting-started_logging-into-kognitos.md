# Logging In | Kognitos Documentation

#### hashtagNavigate to Login

Navigate to the Kognitos login page from your web browser.

US RegionEU RegionUK Region

#### hashtagEnter Your Email

Enter the email address associated with your Kognitos account and clickContinue.

Alternatively, you can log in with Google or Microsoft.

#### hashtagCheck Your Email

Check your inbox for an email from Kognitos containing a 6-digitverification code.

#### hashtagEnter the Verification Code

Return to the login page, enter your verification code, then clickContinue. The code will verify your identity and allow you to proceed.

#### hashtagAccess Your Account

Once you’ve entered the code, you’ll be logged into the Kognitos platform.
