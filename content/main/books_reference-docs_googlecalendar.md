### hashtagContinue with Google

To connect to theGoogle Calendarbook, click onContinue with Google. This will redirect you to Google's secure authentication page where you can sign in with your Google account and authorize the application. Once connected, the Kognitos agent will have access to your Google Calendar while keeping your credentials secure.
