## Episode Description

Nearly one year after Generative AI became mainstream, lingering questions still exist on how executives should incorporate this technology into their strategy. Dr. Ramon Lecuona, Assistant Professor of Business Administration for Tuck School of Business, joins host Drayton Wade to share his expert opinion and advice on the topic. As the landscape of technology continues to evolve, Dr. Lecuona emphasizes the importance of executives having a wider view of competition and the business industry overall.

Join us as we discuss:The impact of AI on supplier-buyer relationshipsThe formation of new partnerships and ecosystemsThe transition from policy to business
