## hashtagGetting Support

### hashtagUsing KAIA (In-App Chat)

KAIA(Kognitos AI Assistant) is available directly within the Kognitos app. KAIA automatically generates answers based on previous cases, documentation, and knowledge articles.

If KAIA's response doesn't resolve your issue, you can say"connect to an agent","create case", or"talk to a person"to automatically create a support case. You can then track the case in the Support Portal.

### hashtagAccessing the Support Portal

#### hashtagNavigate to the Portal

Go tosupport.kognitos.comarrow-up-right.

#### hashtagLog In

ClickLogin Using Kognitosand enter your registered email from the Kognitos app.

#### hashtagVerify Your Email

Enter the verification code sent to your email address. After verification, you'll be taken to the Support Portal homepage(provided you have access).

## hashtagManaging Cases

### hashtagViewing Cases

In theCasessection, your open cases are displayed by default. To view closed cases or all organization cases, click onMy Open Casesand select a different list view.

### hashtagCommunicating on a Case

Use theCommentssection to communicate with the support team. Type your message in the "Share an update" box and clickShare. Support agents will respond in the same thread. All email communication is also visible in the Comments section.

### hashtagEscalating a Case

If a case is not progressing, you can escalate it directly from the portal to prioritize resolution.

If you have trouble logging in to the Support Portal, please contact our support team.
