### hashtagVersions

Books areversionedto ensure stability and reliability. You can choose to upgrade at your own pace, gaining access to enhancements without risking disruptions to your workflows.

Our newest books are built with our Book Development Kit(BDK).All Booksdisplays the latest version of BDK books, including their numeric version numbers(e.g., 2.0.1).

Older books that are still actively maintained are labeled with aLegacytag. In some cases, both the latest BDK version and the legacy version will be available for the same book.

We always recommend using thelatestversion(BDK)for new automation workflows.

### hashtagBook Details

Click on any book to access comprehensive details about it. TheOverviewsection includes information on authentication methods, versioning, and support status. Books built with our Book Development Kit(BDK)also feature anActionstab that showcases all available procedures with usage examples.

Actions are currently only available forBDKbooks.

### hashtagInstallation

Installingor connecting a book enables the agent to understand and execute the automation procedures defined within the book. AnInstalloption will appear on theBook Detailspage(for books that do not require authentication).

Books that require authentication are installed by setting up aconnection.

TheInstalledtab withinAll Booksshows the books you've installed or connected. This section allows you to view what you're already using in your agent.
